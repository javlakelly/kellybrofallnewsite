  • SOS Children's Villages

    How to get 25-30 years old to fundraise?

    SOS Children's Villages is an independent, non-governmental international development organisation that protects the interests and rights of children. The brief was how to get 25-30 years old to fundraise money to the organization, with a digital solution.

  • We did

    The option to donate in an easy way

    An app that helps a person to save money that they usually spend on unnecessary things, yet it also gives them the option to donate in an easy way and contributes with the knowledge what money actually can be worth for someone in need.

  • Process

    People have the ambition and economy to donate, but few actually do it.

    A one week project where we collected interviews and find that most people have the ambition and economy to donate, but few actually does it. Donating money to a cause is usually a satisfying experience but it’s seldom fun. As a consequence people tend to postpone their donation, which in worst case could lead to no donation at all.

  • Storytelling & Visualization

    What does the reader needs to know?

    With a dataset of 3000 people that has answered about their sex life, we were given to find insights and tell it with a story. One of the challenges(/Brief) is give the what the reader needs to know.

  • We did

    A fun and beautiful storytelling with real errors

    A website where we present different parameters of what does impact your masturbation. We want you to question your own prejudices.

  • Process

    You start to question everything

    This project demanded a lot of work with the data. We even got to a point where we started to question the data so much so we did an extra calculation, SEM (Standard Error of the Mean). During this process we have realized that what ever infographic or visualisation that is presented, you should always question it. Are the numbers well represented?

  • Halebop

    Measuring the right numbers?

    Halebop, a telecom company in Sweden, has asked us if they are measuring the right numbers when it comes to sales.

  • We did

    A strategic and tactical plan

    We gave a strategical and tactical plan of how they should measure in growth, rather then just sales and the importance of listen to the customers voice. We gave them a bigger picture of how the business is going for all the departments.

  • Process

    Insights about growth

    Investigating their Google Analytics settings and numbers has taken most of the time, and the last three weeks we worked very much with all different processes because of the insights we got about growth from air bnb, during a lecture.
